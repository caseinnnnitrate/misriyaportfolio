Services

Expert day-to-day support for CXOs and senior leaders, including confidential meeting coordination, calendar management, and communication across teams to ensure all business and personal admin runs smoothly.

Preparation, editing, and management of executive correspondence, business reports, presentations, and sensitive documentation with accuracy and discretion.

Coordination between HR and departments, handling employee queries, absence records, recruitment logistics, onboarding, offboarding, and enforcement of compliance with HR policies.

Management of internal/external meetings, Board and committee sessions, workshops, and special events, including all logistics, scheduling, resource booking, and report distribution.

Ad-hoc & Crisis Management

Immediate response to unforeseen issues, urgent requests, and last-minute assignments, providing creative solutions and ensuring uninterrupted executive operations.
